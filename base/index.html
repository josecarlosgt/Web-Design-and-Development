Explore Laguna Brava

Kayaking

Cross the four kilometers in a kayak guided by a local guide. This guided visit is physically demanding, and we recommend it for only those in excellent physical condition.

  • Duration: 3 hours approx.
  • Price: $40 USD
  • Minimum: 1 pax

Boat Rides

Board a rowing boat that takes to around the most beautiful parts of the lake. This guided visit does not require physical effort.

  • Duration: 3 hours approx.
  • Price: $40 USD
  • Minimum: 4 pax
